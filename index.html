Tong Wu 武桐

PhD Candidate | Formal Verification & Machine Learning

I am a PhD student in the S3 Group at the University of Manchester, supervised by Prof. Lucas C. Cordeiro and Dr. Pavlos Petoumenos, fully funded by EPSRC and Arm Centre of Excellence. My research interests focus on formal verification and machine learning for verifying the safety of concurrent software.

  • JCWIT: A Correctness-Witness Validator for Java Programs Based on Bounded Model Checking [ISSTA 2024]
